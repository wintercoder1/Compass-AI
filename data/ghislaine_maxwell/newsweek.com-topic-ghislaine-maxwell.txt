Ghislaine Maxwell

Updates on Ghislaine Maxwell. A former British socialite, Maxwell was found guilty of child sex trafficking in 2021 in connection to her relationship with sex offender and former financer Jeffrey Epstein. The daughter of Robert Maxwell, the British media mogul and former member of parliament, Ghislaine Maxwell moved from France to New York City in 1991, shortly after her father's death. She met Epstein in the early 90s and the two briefly had a relationship. After his 2008 conviction for soliciting a minor, Maxwell distanced herself from Epstein.
